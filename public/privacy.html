Privacy Policy for SprinklerSmart

Effective Date: [DATE]

Desert Services Group LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the SprinklerSmart mobile application and related web services ("App").

SprinklerSmart is designed with privacy in mind. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not maintain a server-side user database of your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data. The App may request access to your device's GPS location. This is used only locally within the App to center the map on your property so you can plan your irrigation system.

Purchase Information. SprinklerSmart offers a one-time "Pro Plan" PDF export for $19. We use Stripe to securely process payments. We do not collect, process, or store your credit card details.

Usage Data. We collect anonymous, non-personally-identifying usage events (session_start, plan_designed, pro_plan_initiated, pro_plan_purchased, affiliate_clicked). We do not collect your email, name, or device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Location data centers the map on your property. Anonymous usage events help us understand how the App is used and improve it. Payment data facilitates your Pro Plan purchase, handled entirely by Stripe.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not sell your data and do not use third-party advertising SDKs. We use:

  • Stripe — secure, PCI-compliant payment processing.
  • PostHog — anonymous usage analytics, no personally identifiable information.
  • Amazon Associates — "Shop this plan" links go to Amazon via a standard affiliate link; we share no personal data with Amazon.

4. Data Retention

Location data is used only during your active session and is never stored server-side. Anonymous usage events are retained by PostHog to help us improve the App.

5. Your Rights

Because we do not collect personally identifiable information, there is no personal data linked to your identity for us to access, correct, or delete. Uninstalling the App stops all data collection.

6. Children's Privacy

SprinklerSmart is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.
