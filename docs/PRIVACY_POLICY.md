# Privacy Policy for SprinklerSmart

**Effective Date:** [DATE]

Desert Services Group LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the SprinklerSmart mobile application and related web services ("App").

SprinklerSmart is designed with privacy in mind. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not maintain a server-side user database of your personal information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Location Data
The App may request access to your device's GPS location (via device location services). This is used **only** locally within the App to center the map on your property so you can plan your irrigation system.

### B. Purchase Information
SprinklerSmart offers a one-time "Pro Plan" PDF export available for $19. We use Stripe to securely process these payments. We do not collect, process, or store your credit card numbers or payment details.

### C. Usage Data
We collect anonymous, non-personally-identifying usage events (such as `session_start`, `plan_designed`, `pro_plan_initiated`, `pro_plan_purchased`, and `affiliate_clicked`). We do not collect your email, name, or device identifiers. There is no distinct per-user ID linked to your identity beyond a generic app-level identifier.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- **App Functionality:** Your location data is used strictly in-session to provide the core map-centering functionality of the App.
- **Analytics:** Anonymous usage events are used to understand how users interact with the App and to improve our services.
- **Payment Processing:** To facilitate your purchase of the Pro Plan PDF export.

## 3. Third-Party Services

We do not sell your data. We use the following third-party services, which may process your data according to their own privacy policies. We do not use third-party advertising SDKs.

- **Stripe:** Used for secure, PCI-compliant payment processing. Stripe operates off-device and handles all payment data.
- **PostHog:** Used to collect and analyze anonymous usage events. No personally identifiable information is sent to PostHog.
- **Amazon Associates:** When you click a "Shop this plan" product link, you will be directed to Amazon. We do not share any personal data with Amazon; this is a standard affiliate tracking link.

## 4. Data Retention

- **Location Data:** Location data is used only during your active session and is not stored server-side.
- **Usage Data:** Anonymous analytics events are retained by our analytics provider (PostHog) to help us improve the App.

## 5. Your Rights

Because we do not collect personally identifiable information (such as your name, email, or account details), there is no personal data linked to your identity for us to access, correct, or delete. If you wish to stop all anonymous data collection, simply uninstall the App.

## 6. Children's Privacy

SprinklerSmart is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
