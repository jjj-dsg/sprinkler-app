SprinklerSmart requires no account or login — the app is fully usable immediately on launch. It requests device location (When In Use) only to center the satellite map on the reviewer's/user's property; denying it still allows full manual use via the city/address selector. The "Get PDF Blueprint — $19" button opens a Stripe-hosted checkout page in the device browser/webview (Safari), not a native purchase — SprinklerSmart sells a one-time downloadable PDF document (a personalized irrigation blueprint), not digital content consumed inside the app.
